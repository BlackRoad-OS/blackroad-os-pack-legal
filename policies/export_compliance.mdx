# Export Compliance Policy

This policy outlines how {{ company_name }} complies with U.S. and international export control and sanctions laws.

## Classification and Screening
Classify products and technology under applicable Export Control Classification Numbers. Screen customers and partners against restricted party lists before engagement.

## Embargoed Regions
Do not provide services or support to embargoed countries or prohibited end users. Maintain records of determinations for at least five (5) years.

## Technology Transfers
Obtain necessary licenses prior to exporting controlled technology, including transfers via remote access or downloads.

## Training and Oversight
Compliance training is required for personnel with access to controlled technology. Managers must enforce this policy and document exceptions.

## Incident Response
Report potential violations immediately to {{ compliance_officer }}. Suspend transactions pending investigation.

## Recordkeeping
Maintain export-related records, including licenses and screening results, consistent with regulatory retention requirements.
