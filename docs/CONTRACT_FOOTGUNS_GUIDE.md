# Contract Footguns Guide

This guide highlights conceptual risk areas to raise with counsel. It is not jurisdiction-specific and does not guarantee sufficiency.

## Alignment Between SOW and MSA
- Ensure statements of work do not override key protections in the master agreement.
- Watch for conflicting order of precedence provisions.

## Auto-Renewal Clauses
- Identify renewal mechanics and notice periods.
- Confirm escalation paths before auto-renewal triggers.

## Indemnity Scope and Caps
- Watch for uncapped indemnities or broad defense obligations.
- Validate caps align to the commercial relationship.

## IP Assignments and Licenses
- Look for broad assignments that may swallow pre-existing IP.
- Confirm license scope is clear and limited to intended use.

## Data Protection Addenda
- Ensure data processing details match actual data flows.
- Flag cross-border transfer mechanisms for counsel review.

## Service Level Credits
- Confirm credits are aligned with remedies and do not waive other rights.

Always route final decisions and drafting to qualified counsel.
