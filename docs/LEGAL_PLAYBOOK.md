# Legal & Policy Pack Playbook

## What the Legal & Policy Pack Is (and Is Not)
- Provides structured templates, outlines, and issue-spotting helpers.
- Integrates with the Agent Catalog and Packs system for repeatable legal-ops workflows.
- **Is not** a source of legal advice, compliance determinations, or enforceable documents.

## When to Use It
- Preparing for contract drafting and redlines before sending to counsel.
- Running lightweight internal reviews of policies, terms of service, or privacy notices.
- Documenting changes and keeping a clear history of updates for stakeholders.

## How to Work with Human Counsel
- Use the outputs here as a checklist and briefing package for licensed attorneys.
- Route any jurisdiction-specific questions to counsel; do not rely on automated answers.
- Keep human review in the loop for approvals, risk decisions, and final wording.

## Connections to BlackRoad OS
- **blackroad-os-docs**: host policies, terms, and outlines for collaboration.
- **blackroad-os-archive**: manage artifacts like trademarks, domains, and signed documents.
- **blackroad-os-api**: invoke agents and workflows programmatically from operators.

## Usage Notes
- All workflows include `not_legal_advice: true` to reinforce boundaries.
- Templates are skeletal outlines to facilitate discussion, not ready-to-sign documents.

## How to Engage Human Counsel
1. Generate an outline or issue-spotting summary using this pack.
2. Package the output with business context and questions for counsel.
3. Confirm final terms, governing law, and risk allocation through licensed attorneys.

## Example Workflows
- **Contract Review**: Draft an outline, highlight risks, and assemble questions for counsel.
- **Policy Update**: Summarize differences, log changes, and propose communication plans.
- **DPA Risk Assessment**: Review DPA checklist completion and prompt counsel questions.

Always reiterate: these tools assist preparation and documentation only—they do not replace legal advice.
