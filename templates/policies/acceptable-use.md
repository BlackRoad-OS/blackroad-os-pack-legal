### 1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") describes the permitted and prohibited uses of **[COMPANY NAME]**'s services ("Services"). This AUP is incorporated into and forms part of the Terms of Service.

**Purpose:** To ensure fair, safe, and lawful use of our Services for all users.

**Effective Date:** [DATE]  
**Last Updated:** [DATE]

---

### 2. Acceptable Uses ✅

You MAY use our Services to:

- [ ] Access and use features as intended
- [ ] Store and process lawful content
- [ ] Collaborate with authorized users
- [ ] Develop applications using our APIs (per API terms)
- [ ] [OTHER PERMITTED USES]

---

### 3. Prohibited Uses 🚫

You may NOT use our Services to:

#### 3.1 Illegal Activities
- [ ] Violate any applicable laws or regulations
- [ ] Facilitate illegal transactions
- [ ] Store or transmit illegal content
- [ ] Engage in fraudulent activities

#### 3.2 Harmful Content
- [ ] Distribute malware, viruses, or harmful code
- [ ] Send spam or unsolicited communications
- [ ] Host phishing content
- [ ] Distribute content that promotes violence or harm

#### 3.3 Abuse of Service
- [ ] Attempt to gain unauthorized access
- [ ] Interfere with Service availability
- [ ] Excessive use that impacts other users
- [ ] Circumvent security measures
- [ ] Use automated systems without permission (scraping, bots)

#### 3.4 Intellectual Property
- [ ] Infringe copyrights, trademarks, or patents
- [ ] Use without proper licensing
- [ ] Misappropriate trade secrets

#### 3.5 Privacy Violations
- [ ] Collect personal data without consent
- [ ] Violate privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- [ ] Dox or expose private information

#### 3.6 Harmful Business Practices
- [ ] Impersonate others
- [ ] Engage in deceptive practices
- [ ] Defame or harass others

### 5. Security Requirements

You agree to:

- [ ] Use strong, unique passwords
- [ ] Enable multi-factor authentication (if available)
- [ ] Keep credentials confidential
- [ ] Report security incidents promptly
- [ ] Maintain security of your systems

### 6. Reporting Violations

If you observe a violation of this AUP:

**Report to:** [ABUSE EMAIL]  
**Include:**
- Description of the violation
- Evidence (screenshots, URLs, logs)
- Date and time observed
- Any other relevant information

**We will:**
- [ ] Acknowledge receipt within [X] hours
- [ ] Investigate the report
- [ ] Take appropriate action
- [ ] Notify reporter of outcome (where appropriate)

---

### 7. Enforcement

Violations of this AUP may result in:

| Severity | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Minor / First offense | Warning notification |
| Moderate / Repeat offense | Temporary suspension |
| Severe / Continued violation | Permanent termination |
| Illegal activity | Termination + legal action |

**We reserve the right to:**
- [ ] Remove violating content
- [ ] Suspend or terminate accounts
- [ ] Report illegal activities to authorities
- [ ] Pursue legal remedies
