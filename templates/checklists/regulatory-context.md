**Customer Identification Program (CIP):**
- [ ] Identity verification process documented
- [ ] Acceptable ID types defined
- [ ] Verification methods implemented
- [ ] Record retention policy established

**Know Your Customer (KYC):**
- [ ] Customer due diligence (CDD) procedures
- [ ] Enhanced due diligence (EDD) for high-risk customers
- [ ] Beneficial ownership identification
- [ ] Ongoing monitoring procedures

**Anti-Money Laundering (AML):**
- [ ] AML policy documented
- [ ] Transaction monitoring implemented
- [ ] Suspicious activity reporting (SAR) procedures
- [ ] Sanctions screening (OFAC, EU, UN lists)
- [ ] PEP screening
- [ ] AML officer designated
- [ ] Staff training program

**Record Keeping:**
- [ ] Minimum 5-year retention
- [ ] Records accessible for examination
- [ ] Audit trail maintained
