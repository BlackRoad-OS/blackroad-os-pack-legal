# Master Services Agreement Outline

> NOTE: This is a structural outline for discussion with counsel. It is NOT a legally sufficient agreement on its own.

## Scope of Services
- Deliverables, milestones, and service levels
- Acceptance criteria and change management

## Term & Termination
- Initial term and renewal options
- Termination for cause and for convenience

## Fees & Payment
- Pricing model, invoicing schedule, and taxes
- Late payment handling and credits

## IP Ownership
- Pre-existing IP vs. new deliverables
- License grants and restrictions

## Confidentiality
- Definition of confidential information
- Exclusions, duration, and destruction/return

## Data Protection
- Security measures and incident response
- Data Processing Addendum requirements if applicable

## Warranties / Disclaimers
- Limited warranties aligned to services
- Disclaimers of implied warranties

## Indemnity
- Indemnification scope and procedures
- Defense and settlement mechanics

## Limitation of Liability
- Caps and exclusions (e.g., indirect damages)

## Governing Law / Venue
- Chosen law and forum (confirm with counsel)

## Miscellaneous
- Assignment, notices, entire agreement, order of precedence
